Using your latitude and longitude, How Much Sunlight tells you the difference between now and the nearest dawn or dusk.

Dawn is separate from sunrise, and dusk is separate from sunset. At sunrise and sunset, the top edge of the sun is just barely visible over the horizon; at dawn and dusk, the sun is below the horizon, but there is enough light to see by.

Please note that these calculations are approximate, and, while they are more or less accurate, should not be considered reliable for serious situations.
